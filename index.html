The Opportunity

Why Central Florida Is the Market Your Brand Needs

Central Florida isn't just a regional market — it's one of the most dynamic spirits environments in the country. A relentless flow of tourism, a rapidly growing permanent population, and a thriving on-premise scene create an opportunity that few markets can match.

Florida is one of the three largest spirits markets in the United States. Central Florida alone captures 38% of all Florida liquor sales — making it the single most consequential market in the state for brand growth.

About iHospitality

Hospitality Is in Our Blood

Phil King didn't stumble into the beverage industry — he grew up in it.

Starting as a classically trained chef, Phil spent 15 years in professional kitchens designing menus and managing back-of-house operations before making a deliberate move into wine and spirits in 2000. That culinary foundation gives iHospitality a perspective most brokers simply don't have: a deep, firsthand understanding of how beverages function within the full context of a hospitality experience.

Over two decades in the industry, Phil built relationships with the account owners and operators who trust a recommendation because they know it comes from real expertise — not just a sales pitch.

2000

Joined Southern Wine & Spirits, one of the nation's largest distributors, launching a career in beverage sales.

2004

Moved to Premier Beverage, earning recognition for award-winning on-premise fine wine sales performance.

2009

Promoted into Spirits Division management — progressing from Key Accounts to Account Development to Craft Spirit Specialist over the following decade.

Today

Founded iHospitality to give craft spirit brands the dedicated, expert representation they deserve in Central Florida's booming market.
